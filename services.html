Vegetarian

Masala Dosa

Vegetarian

Rava Dosa

Vegetarian

Onion Dosa

Paper Dosa

Plain Idli

Vegetarian

Rava Idli

Vegetarian

Medu Vada

Crispy, savory doughnut-shaped fritters made from urad dal, served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Idli Sambar

Podi Idli

Masala Vada

Vegetarian

Crispy fritters made from chana dal (split chickpeas), flavored with onions, chilies, and spices.

Curries & Side Dishes

Sambar

Vegetarian

Lentil-based vegetable stew flavored with tamarind and sambar powder, a staple in South Indian cuisine.

Rasam

Vegetarian

Tangy, spicy soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, pepper, and other spices, known for its digestive properties.

Avial

Vegetarian

Mixed vegetables cooked with coconut, yogurt, and curry leaves, a Kerala specialty.

Poriyal

Vegetarian

Stir-fried vegetables seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and grated coconut.

Kootu

Vegetarian

Vegetables and lentils cooked together with coconut and spices, a nutritious side dish.

Pachadi

Vegetarian

Yogurt-based side dish with vegetables or fruits, tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Desserts

Payasam

Vegetarian

Sweet pudding made with milk, rice or vermicelli, sugar, and flavored with cardamom and nuts.

Mysore Pak

Vegetarian

Rich, fudge-like sweet made from gram flour, ghee, and sugar, a specialty from Mysore.

Jalebi

Vegetarian

Deep-fried pretzel-shaped sweets soaked in sugar syrup, crispy on the outside and syrupy inside.

Gulab Jamun

Vegetarian

Soft, spongy milk-solid balls soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup, a popular dessert for celebrations.

Rasmalai

Vegetarian

Soft cottage cheese dumplings soaked in sweetened, thickened milk flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Laddu

Vegetarian

Sweet, round balls made from flour, sugar, and ghee, often flavored with nuts and cardamom.

Beverages

Filter Coffee

Vegetarian

Traditional South Indian coffee made with freshly ground coffee beans and chicory, served with milk and sugar.

Masala Chai

Vegetarian

Spiced tea made with a blend of aromatic spices, tea leaves, milk, and sugar.

Buttermilk (Chaas)

Vegetarian

Refreshing yogurt-based drink flavored with cumin, curry leaves, and coriander, perfect for hot days.

Lassi

Vegetarian

Creamy yogurt-based drink that can be sweet or savory, often flavored with fruits or spices.

Fresh Coconut Water

Vegetarian

Natural, refreshing drink served directly from tender coconuts, rich in electrolytes.

Fresh Fruit Juices

Vegetarian

Variety of freshly squeezed seasonal fruit juices including mango, pineapple, watermelon, and more.
